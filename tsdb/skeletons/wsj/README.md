The Wall Street Journal (WSJ) sentences are from the Penn Treebank, and
consists of articles published between January 1987 and June 1989 in
the Dow Jones News Service/Wall Street Journal articles.

LDC identifiers include LDC93T3A (Penn Treebank Release 2) and others;
the WSJ section is about 1 million words.

## The Standard Splits

* Training: Sections 02–21 (~40,000 sentences)
* Development: Section 22 (~1,700 sentences)
* Test: Section 23 (~2,400 sentences)

Sections 00–01 are often kept aside for experiments, prototyping, or
extra training, and are not part of the standard splits.
